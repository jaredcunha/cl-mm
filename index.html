Frequently Asked Questions

I’m already an LS merchant, do I need to re-enroll in order to participate in Restaurants Plus?

Yep, but it’s super-easy. Just head here and follow the instructions.

How does Restaurants Plus work?

It’s simple: A consumer joins our Restaurants Plus program by linking a credit or debit card to our system. When the card is used at a partnering restaurant, we’ll reward the cardholder with cash-back savings.

As a merchant, you’ll have control over how much and when to discount, which means you can set higher discounts at specific days and times to encourage business when it’s slow. Pretty sweet, right?

Other than that, no additional work is required from you or your staff. The savings are automatic—no vouchers, no hassle.

Each month, LivingSocial will provide a detailed report on the ROI of the program, including revenue and number of customers (new and returning). The report also highlights marketing metrics such as email mentions, search views, and app/web views. You’ll be able to track exactly how much business Restaurants Plus is helping you bring in.

Is Restaurants Plus an additional charge for existing customers?

No, there will be no charge for customers. They will, however, be required (free of charge) to register for Restaurants Plus at LivingSocial via desktop or mobile applications.

What is a Cash Reward?

Once consumers reach a $10 payback balance, they’re eligible for a monthly payment in the amount they’ve accrued. Payment is   automatically credited back to the activated payback card. If their account balance has not yet reached $10, payment rolls over to the next month (or until the balance reaches $10).

Why is this appealing to customers?

As easy as it is for you, it’s even easier for customers. They’re going to discover great new restaurants throughout the area–and save cash when they visit them. Plus, they’ll receive these savings automatically on their next billing statement. No fiddling with phones or paper vouchers—just eat, pay, and save.

Do I have to pay any additional fees to LS?

There are no upfront fees to join the network and there is a monthly fee that varies depending on how much traffic is being sent to you.
